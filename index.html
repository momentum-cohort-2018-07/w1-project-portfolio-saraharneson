About Me

I grew up in Montclair, New Jersey, and moved to Chapel Hill for college. I graduated from UNC with a degree in journalism (graphic design concentration) and English literature. I was the associate art director at the Carolina Alumni Review, then senior art director for Durham Magazine, Chapel Hill Magazine, and Chatham Magazine, and I also shot for those publications. I freelance in both design and photography. After a career in print, I'm finally learning to code!
